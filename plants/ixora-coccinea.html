Notes

Ixora coccinea is often confused with Ixora chinensis as they have very similar flowers. However you can distinguish them by the petals. Ixora coccinea has pointy petals, while the ones of Ixora chinensis are much more rounded.

In KAUST Ixora coccinea is planted all over the campus as ornamental plants in gardens or in front of houses.
